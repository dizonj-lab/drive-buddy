# DRIVE BUDDY

Drive Buddy, is a co-pilot safety system employing Eye Aspect Ratio (EAR) Logic and Machine Learning to detect drowsiness through Eye Aspect Ratio analysis. Using Image Recognition system, the solution aims detect fatigue markers as well other eye distractions. This study provide details of the conceptual design and basic design for the development of this solution. The study reviews notable research works conducted in the area of Eye Aspect Ratio and how to leverage on EAR to measure fatigues. These research studies will layout the foundation of this development study and use the research concept, outcomes and challenges to develop a practical solution. Furthermore, the later part of this focuses on the “how” of the solution development will came to be, the methods to be use, the development plan, and timeline to bring the solution to fruition.
